What is Science and Spiritual?

Science and spirituality, though distinct, can be complementary.Science helps us understand the how of the universe,while spirituality can help us explore the why.This club is a place to discuss the big questions about existence: Where did we come from? What is our purpose? Is there something more? Explore how scientific discoveries can inform our spiritual understanding, and vice versa.Learn about different scientific and spiritual traditions. Hear from guest speakers who are scientists or spiritual leaders engaged in this dialogue.

ABOUT US

Omega Science and spirituality is the life style club of Natioanl Institute Of Technology Tirchirappalli run by students focusing on the mental welfare of the students of the institute.This club is more diverted towards day to day life affairs. Declining the idea of renunciation totally this club supports living a family life and learning values through experiences.
